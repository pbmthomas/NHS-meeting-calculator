NHS Meeting Cost Calculator

2025/26 pay scales · England · AfC / Consultant / VSM

This tool calculates how much an NHS meeting costs based on who attends and how long it lasts. It uses the official 2025/26 pay scales for England. You don't need to install anything. No data is sent anywhere — everything stays on your device.

  1. Choose your mode
    Pick Live meeting to run a timer during a meeting happening now, or Past meeting to enter how long a previous meeting lasted.
  2. Add each person who attended
    Use a quick-fill template to load a common set of attendees in one click, or add people individually. For each person, pick their pay framework (AfC for most staff, Consultant for doctors, VSM for board members), their band, and an optional uplift % (e.g. 20 for extra PAs). Click + Add and repeat.
  3. Check settings
    On-costs adds the employer NI, pension and levy (~35%) — this is the true cost to the trust. London weighting adds HCAS if your trust is in London.
  4. For a live meeting — press Start. The cost ticks up in real time.
    Share your screen during a Teams call to show the running total.
  5. For a past meeting — type in the hours and minutes. The cost updates instantly.
  6. Use the summary
    See total cost, what it equates to in clinical activity, and the annual cost if this meeting recurs. Use Copy summary to paste into an email, Print/PDF to save, or preview a Teams notification card.

Jargon buster

Agenda for Change (AfC)The NHS pay system for most staff. Has 9 bands.
Band (e.g. Band 7)A pay grade within AfC. Higher = more senior.
VSMVery Senior Manager — board-level roles (CEO, CFO, COO, etc.).
Threshold (1–5)Consultant pay levels based on years of experience.
Uplift %Extra percentage on base salary, e.g. 20% for additional PAs.
On-costs (~35%)Employer NI + NHS pension + levy. The true cost to the trust.
HCASHigh Cost Area Supplement — London weighting (up to 20%).
